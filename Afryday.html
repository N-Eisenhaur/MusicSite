Amber Fryday

Amber is currently enrolled in the Radio Television Journalism program at NSCC. Amber has had a love for all things art and media since she was a child and spent many years involved in musical theatre.

Music is still a huge part of her life and Amber is currently a member of the Nova Scotia Mass Choir.

Amber works diligently to produce great content for our artists. She will be traveling to Tanzania, Africa, later this year to produce website content for Farm Radio.
