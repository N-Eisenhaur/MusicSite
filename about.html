About Us:

Established in 2019, Pryor Management formalized as an operating group. The principals of the team have over 25 years of combined experience in the music industry in Atlantic Canada. Pryor Management was developed to provide a quality service to artists that are both established and those starting out who want effective management and booking services. Pryor Management prides itself in providing services to meet the needs of artists from booking to event management.
